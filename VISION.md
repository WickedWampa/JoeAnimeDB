\# 🎯 JoeAnimeDB Vision



\## Our Mission



Build the anime companion we've always wanted.



Not another streaming service.



Not another MyAnimeList.



Not another AniList.



Something personal.



\---



\## The Core Idea



Streaming services remember where you stopped watching.



JoeAnimeDB remembers your anime journey.



\---



\## Everything We Build Should Answer One Question



Does this help the app understand the user better?



If yes...



Build it.



If not...



It probably doesn't belong.



\---



\## Philosophy



JoeAnimeDB should learn:



\- what you enjoy

\- what you dislike

\- your favorite genres

\- your favorite studios

\- what deserves a rewatch

\- what should come next



The app grows smarter the longer you use it.



\---



\## Recommendation Philosophy



Most apps ask:



"What anime is popular?"



JoeAnimeDB asks:



"What anime will THIS user love?"



Recommendations should always explain themselves.



Example:



Because you loved:



• Bleach

• World Trigger

• Initial D



You might enjoy:



• Kingdom



Reason:



Strategic battles, long character development, large ensemble cast.



\---



\## AI Philosophy



AI is not the product.



JoeAnimeDB is.



The database is the brain.



AI is simply another way to interact with it.



The application should remain useful even without AI.



\---



\## Long-Term Goal



Become the best desktop anime companion available.



Private.



Fast.



Offline capable.



Beautiful.



Built around the user instead of popularity.



\---



\## One Sentence



JoeAnimeDB doesn't try to predict what the world will love.



It learns what you love.
