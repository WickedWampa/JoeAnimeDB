# 🍜 JoeAnimeDB

> Your Anime. Your History. Your Taste.

JoeAnimeDB is a desktop anime companion built for people who have watched hundreds of anime and want something more personal than MyAnimeList or Crunchyroll history.

Instead of tracking what everyone likes...

JoeAnimeDB learns **what YOU like.**

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎨 Modern desktop UI
- 💾 SQLite database
- ❤️ Favorites
- ⭐ Personal scoring
- 📚 Personal notes
- 🔁 Rewatch tracking
- 🎭 Multiple themes
- 🖼 Automatic artwork
- 📊 Dashboard
- 🔍 Fast search
- 📈 Rankings

## 🎯 Vision

JoeAnimeDB isn't trying to replace streaming services.

Streaming services remember
what episode you're on.

JoeAnimeDB remembers
your entire anime journey.

It answers questions like:

• What have I watched?
• What did I think?
• What deserves a rewatch?
• What should I watch next?
• Did a new season release?

Recommendations are built around
your taste—not popularity.
